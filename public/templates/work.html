Speaker Programs

  • Human Behavior 101

    Coach Kim teaches a hilarious version of Human Behavior and entertains participants while teaching them how to understand people and behave better themselves. Get ready to laugh and learn.

  • Policies and Procedures for a better life

    It is time to re-write your personal policies to create the life you really want. Coach Kim entertains participants while helping them change the way they experience life by changing their official policies.

  • Fear Factor Live

    Coach Kim facilitates an exciting on-stage version of the popular TV show with scary and disgusting stunts that help participants get past their fears. You have to see this to believe it.

  • Dealing with Difficult People

    Coach Kim entertains participants with a humorous and unique take on getting along with other people. You will learn things about human nature that will change you forever.

  • Self Esteem Workshop

    You will leave this event feeling different about yourself. Coach Kim makes participants see themselves accurately for the first time and leaves them laughing too. This is a life changing event.

  • You are Amazing

    (program for youth)

    You are a superhero. You just didn't know it. This program inspires young people to be a force for good in the world and gain amazing self-esteem.
